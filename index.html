Welcome to Auora

What does Auora do?

We create small, fun, and practical software tools and share them with those who appreciate them. We focus on passion projects that enhance everyday digital experiences.

Who are the developers behind Auora?

Auora is maintained by a small team of two developers who are passionate about software creation and problem-solving.

How can I get involved?

We are always open to suggestions on software to create or bugs to squash. If you want to reach out to us, you can in our AuoraWare Discord

Open source?

Unfortunately, we are not open source due to the fact that Auora is maintained by 2 single developers who put a lot of time and effort into creating our software. If we were open source, people would steal our code and claim it as their own.

Malware or detections?

Many popular software developers pay to get a signature from Microsoft to avoid false detections on their software. But this signing can cost upward of $70 to $300 per year. Because of that, we choose not to sign our software. Any antivirus detections on our software are classified as "false positives" due to certain behaviors that may trigger alerts.

Where can I download your software?

You can download our latest software releases from our Downloads page.
